Our Story

Building a world where every student feels at home, no matter where they study.

Why Cozy Corner?

We started Cozy Corner because we've been there. The struggle of moving to a new city, the shady brokers, and the "hostels" that looked nothing like the photos.

We believe students deserve better. A safe space, transparent pricing, and a community that welcomes you. We aren't just a listing site; we are your first friend in a new city.

Our Mission

To eliminate the hassle of student housing by providing verified, affordable, and safe accommodation options near every major campus in India.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted student housing network, where finding a place to stay is as easy as ordering a pizza.
