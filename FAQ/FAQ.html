Frequently Asked Questions

How do I verify if a property is safe?
All properties marked with a "Verified" badge have been physically visited by our team. We check for safety, amenities, and cleanliness before listing.
Do I have to pay brokerage?
No! Cozy Corner is a brokerage-free platform for students. You only pay the rent and a small one-time service fee for legal agreement processing.
Can I book a property without visiting?
Yes, many students book in advance. We provide video tours and detailed photos. However, we always recommend a physical visit if possible.
What documents do I need for renting?
You will typically need your College ID card (or admission letter) and a government ID (Aadhar/PAN) for address proof.
Are guests allowed in PGs?
This depends on the specific host's rules. Please check the "House Rules" section on the property detail page before booking.
