Cozy Corner Safety

Your Safety is Our Priority

Guidelines to ensure a secure and happy stay for everyone.

Fraud Prevention Tips

  • Never pay cash before visiting: Always visit the property or ask for a live video tour before transferring any deposit.
  • Use the App: Keep all conversations and payments within the Cozy Corner platform for record-keeping.
  • Check for "Verified" Badge: Stick to properties that have been verified by our team.
  • Report Suspicious Activity: If a host asks for OTPs or QR code scanning to receive money, report them immediately.
For Students

Respect House Rules

Every host has specific rules regarding timings, guests, and noise. Read them carefully before booking to avoid conflicts.

Keep it Clean

Treat the property like your own home. Maintain hygiene in shared spaces like kitchens and washrooms.

Emergency Contacts

Save local emergency numbers and the Cozy Corner support line (+91 1800-COZY-HELP) on your phone.

For Hosts

Accurate Listings

Do not use fake photos. Misrepresentation leads to immediate account suspension and refund liabilities.

Tenant Verification

Always verify the student's College ID and Government ID before handing over the keys.

Safe Environment

Ensure all locks, electricals, and security systems (CCTV) are functional. Respect student privacy.
